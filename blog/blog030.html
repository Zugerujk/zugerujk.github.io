It's 1:38AM, and by the time I'm done here, daylight savings time will have skipped forward. It's a weird environment, right now. I'm at a booth, with a troupe of women south of me and a family speaking Spanish to the northwest of me. My order just got taken - a berry crepe with scrambled eggs. I've had a craving for Denny's since coming to Minnesota. On the drive up here, it Clicked, and I finally became a changed person from IHOP. The one here is vastly more spacious, and built less like a diner and more like a McDonalds, but that's fine. Things are different everywhere, and right now I'm a 16 hour drive from Houston and the ur-Denny's of my mind.

Just finished eating. And the clock skipped an hour forward. It was pretty good food, and I'm glad I came here to feel all the feelings that come along with the journey here. I felt a lot of shame about halfway through the car ride, but I feel more fine now.
